📋 Project Overview

BiDi is an IT company delivering medical support systems. This database manages:

  • 1000 employees across 5 departments
  • 50 concurrent users (employees + customers)
  • 3 office locations in Finland
  • Dozens of projects simultaneously

🔍 Sample Queries

Simple SELECT

SELECT EmpID, Name, Email, HireDate
FROM Employee
ORDER BY Name;

3-Table JOIN

SELECT e.Name, d.Name AS Dept, l.Address
FROM Employee e
JOIN Department d ON e.DepID = d.DepID
JOIN Location l ON d.LID = l.LID;

Aggregation with HAVING

SELECT d.Name, COUNT(e.EmpID) AS num_emp,
       AVG(e.Salary) AS avg_salary
FROM Department d
LEFT JOIN Employee e ON d.DepID = e.DepID
GROUP BY d.DepID, d.Name
HAVING COUNT(e.EmpID) >= 2;

📊 ER Model

Entities

  • Project (PrID, Name, Budget, Status, CID, startDate, deadline)
  • Customer (CID, Name, Email, LID)
  • Location (LID, Address, Country)
  • Department (DepID, Name, LID)
  • Employee (EmpID, Email, Name, DepID, HireDate, Salary)
  • Role (RoleID, Name)
  • UserGroup (GrID, Name, Description)

Relationships

  • Commissions: Project N:1 Customer (CID in Project)
  • Works: Project M:N Employee
  • Has: Employee M:N Role
  • In: Employee N:1 Department
  • In: Department N:1 Location
  • In: Customer N:1 Location
  • PartOf: UserGroup M:N Employee
